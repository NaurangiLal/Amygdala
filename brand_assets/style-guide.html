Brand & Asset System · v1 · Retro-terminal card club

The single source of truth for Amygdala's visual identity: a green-phosphor CRT world that should feel like one console, not a web app. Every token, effect, and asset here is built from tokens.css. Drop this folder in as brand_assets/.

01Color — phosphor palette

Green on black, with a single amber accent reserved for alerts. Contrast within the green ramp keeps body text readable against the CRT black (WCAG-minded, PRD §6).

02Typography

Three roles: Amoria for display/logo, a clean sans (Space Grotesk) for body, and a pixel face (VT323) for card ranks and HUD. Tight tracking on large headings, generous body line-height.

05The mascot — narrator states

The mascot is the product's voice across the whole UI. One master character maps to eight states, each carrying a data-driven line so content is editable and localizable (PRD §5.5). Mood is expressed through pose, brightness, and the emote badge — never a wall of text.

Full state set: idle · explaining · prompting · thinking · reacting_win · reacting_loss · reacting_neutral · celebrating. Lines shown are examples from the event→state→line table; narration is always skippable and mutable (§5.5, §5.7).

06Cards — the filled-vs-outlined rule

Red vs. black doesn't exist in a monochrome palette, so suit is encoded by fill: spades & clubs render solid, hearts & diamonds render outlined — always beside the rank letter, so a suit is never ambiguous (PRD §5.6).

07Interactive states

Every clickable element carries hover, focus-visible, and active states. Amber is only for destructive or alert actions.

Font note. Amoria is bundled at fonts/Amoria.woff2 (converted from the supplied .otf) and loads via @font-face in tokens.css; the display stack falls back to VT323 if it's ever missing.
